Collection note

About the arrangement

The exhibit is organized across seven chapters, each corresponding to a distinct space encountered during the visit. The sequence moves from gallery rooms focused on natural history, heritage artifacts, and literary portraiture, through open transitional areas, and into the immersive Dáyat installation that closes the experience.

Rather than grouping by medium or date, the arrangement follows the logic of the visit itself — the order in which rooms were entered, the shift in atmosphere from one space to the next, and the cumulative weight of moving through a museum over a full day.
