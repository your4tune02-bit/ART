About the arrangement

The exhibit groups artworks and spaces by theme and atmosphere rather than by chronology alone. Wall studies, heritage-inspired interiors, and immersive installations each form a distinct chapter.

This structure supports both documentation and interpretation by giving each featured work a place within a larger visual and cultural context.
